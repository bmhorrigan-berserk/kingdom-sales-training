Charm EHR: Patient Records, Lab Orders, Billing

Charm is the electronic health record system. Once a prospect becomes a patient (paid, labs ordered), their data moves into Charm. Day 2: Jason will screen-share the Charm walkthrough covering invoicing, billing, lab ordering, and diagnosis codes. Reference the Training Reference Binder for Guides 2 through 5 during this session.

What Sales Needs to Know About Charm

Patient Profiles: Created after $199 collection. Contains demographics, lab results, provider notes, and treatment plans.

Lab Ordering: LabCorp walk-in orders are generated through Charm and sent directly to the patient's email. This is what you are selling: instant access to diagnostic data.

Billing and Recurrings: Recurring treatment charges are managed in Charm. Account Executives handle billing inquiries. You do not discuss specific billing amounts on sales calls.

Scheduling and Calendar: Provider consults are scheduled through Charm. CS team manages the calendar. LMS1 reps bridge the patient to the scheduling step.

Encountering: Providers document each clinical encounter in Charm. This is the medical record. Sales never touches encounter notes.

Charm competency check happens during Week 2. You must understand the patient flow from ZoHo Deal to Charm Patient to treatment delivery.
