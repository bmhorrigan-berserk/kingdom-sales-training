R-5

Core Operating Rules and Compliance

Free Consult Offer: Core hook. Async routing removes the requirement for video but never the option.

Provider Neutrality: Providers NEVER discuss pricing, plans, or costs.

LMS1 Finality: LMS1 converts to paid retainer and scheduled labs. No handoffs before payment.

Information Flow Silos: Pre-patient data stays in Zoho/Sinch. Patient clinical data moves to Charm EHR. HIPAA compliance.

DEA Compliance: All controlled substances require live two-way audiovisual consult per the 2026 extension.

HIPAA and DEA compliance are not optional. Violations put the company, the medical group, and your employment at risk.
