# Kingdom Sales Training - Design Ideas

## Chosen Approach: Military Command Center / Dark Authority

**Design Movement:** Dark Military Command Interface with Clinical Precision
**Core Principles:**
1. Authority through darkness - deep navy and black backgrounds convey command and expertise
2. Amber/gold accents signal urgency and elite status (Kingdom brand warning color)
3. Structured data presentation - information is organized like a mission briefing
4. Kinetic card interactions - flashcards flip with physical weight

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Deep Navy #0D1B2A (darker than brand Deep Blue)
- Primary: Kingdom Deep Blue #2D3580
- Accent: Kingdom Amber #F59E0B (urgency, elite status)
- Text: Crisp white and light gray for maximum readability on dark backgrounds
- Success: Emerald green for correct answers
- Error: Red for incorrect answers

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-screen immersive experience
- Left sidebar navigation for module selection
- Center stage for the active card or quiz question
- Progress bar always visible at top

**Signature Elements:**
1. Crown icon (Kingdom brand) as the logo mark
2. Amber glow effects on active/hover states
3. Card flip animation with 3D perspective for flashcards

**Typography System:**
- Display: Bebas Neue (bold, military authority) for headers
- Body: DM Sans (clean, readable) for content
- Mono: JetBrains Mono for scripts and verbatim quotes

**Animation:**
- Flashcard 3D flip on click (rotateY 180deg)
- Slide-in transitions for quiz questions
- Progress bar smooth fill animation
- Correct/incorrect shake or bounce feedback
